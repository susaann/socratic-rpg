> **Chapter Learning Objectives**
> 
> By the end of this chapter you should be able to:
> 
>   1. Explain why closed-form solutions fail for deep networks and why iterative optimization is necessary
>   2. Derive and compare batch gradient descent, stochastic gradient descent, and mini-batch SGD — including the unbiasedness proof for SGD
>   3. Describe learning rate schedules (step decay, cosine annealing, warmup) and explain the purpose of each
>   4. Derive the Momentum update rule as an exponentially weighted moving average and show analytically why it suppresses oscillations while accelerating consistent directions
>   5. Derive the Adam optimizer from first principles, including the bias-correction argument, and explain what each component contributes
>
