## 6.5 Chapter Summary

Depth provides expressive power through hierarchical composition — each layer refines the representation of the previous layer, enabling exponential parameter efficiency compared to shallow networks. The output layer must be designed to match the task: the output activation encodes the noise model and the loss function is the corresponding negative log-likelihood.

The softmax + cross-entropy gradient ∂L/∂zj=pj−yj\partial L / \partial z_j = p_j - y_j∂L/∂zj​=pj​−yj​ is one of the most elegant results in deep learning: the gradient is simply the predicted probability minus the true indicator, and this formula arises from MLE under a categorical model.

Weight initialization has no free parameters (it is derived from theory) but has decisive practical impact. Zero initialization breaks symmetry and prevents learning entirely. Xavier initialization stabilizes variance for linear activations; He initialization corrects for ReLU's half-zeroing. Both are derived from the requirement that activation variance remains constant through a forward pass of any depth.
