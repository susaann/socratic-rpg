> **Chapter Learning Objectives**
> 
> By the end of this chapter you should be able to:
> 
>   1. State the perceptron model and its learning algorithm, and prove by contradiction that a single-layer perceptron cannot compute XOR
>   2. Prove that a network of LLL linear (non-activation) layers is equivalent to a single linear layer
>   3. Define and differentiate sigmoid, tanh, ReLU, Leaky ReLU, and GELU — including their formulas, derivatives, and primary failure modes
>   4. Write out the MLP forward pass using unified notation and compute the parameter count of a given architecture
>   5. State the Universal Approximation Theorem and explain both its power and its fundamental limitations
