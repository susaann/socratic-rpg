## 4.4 MLP Architecture and Notation

We now establish a unified notation for multi-layer perceptrons (MLPs) that will carry through the rest of this volume.

An **L LL-layer MLP** (L−1L-1L−1 hidden layers plus one output layer) maps input x∈Rd0\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_0}x∈Rd0​ to output y^∈RdL\hat{\mathbf{y}} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_L}y^​∈RdL​ via:

z(l)=W(l)h(l−1)+b(l)(linear pre-activation)\mathbf{z}^{(l)} = \mathbf{W}^{(l)} \mathbf{h}^{(l-1)} + \mathbf{b}^{(l)} \qquad \text{(linear pre-activation)}z(l)=W(l)h(l−1)+b(l)(linear pre-activation) h(l)=σ(l)(z(l))(nonlinear activation)\mathbf{h}^{(l)} = \sigma^{(l)}(\mathbf{z}^{(l)}) \qquad \text{(nonlinear activation)}h(l)=σ(l)(z(l))(nonlinear activation)

for l=1,2,…,Ll = 1, 2, \ldots, Ll=1,2,…,L, with h(0)=x\mathbf{h}^{(0)} = \mathbf{x}h(0)=x and y^=h(L)\hat{\mathbf{y}} = \mathbf{h}^{(L)}y^​=h(L).

**Notation** :

  * dld_ldl​: width (number of units) of layer lll; d0d_0d0​ = input dimension
  * W(l)∈Rdl×dl−1\mathbf{W}^{(l)} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_l \times d_{l-1}}W(l)∈Rdl​×dl−1​: weight matrix at layer lll
  * b(l)∈Rdl\mathbf{b}^{(l)} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_l}b(l)∈Rdl​: bias vector at layer lll
  * σ(l)\sigma^{(l)}σ(l): activation function at layer lll (applied element-wise)



**Total parameter count** :

params=∑l=1Ldl(dl−1+1)=∑l=1L(dl⋅dl−1+dl)\text{params} = \sum_{l=1}^{L} d_l(d_{l-1} + 1) = \sum_{l=1}^{L} (d_l \cdot d_{l-1} + d_l)params=l=1∑L​dl​(dl−1​+1)=l=1∑L​(dl​⋅dl−1​+dl​)

(Each layer contributes dl×dl−1d_l \times d_{l-1}dl​×dl−1​ weight parameters plus dld_ldl​ bias parameters.)

**Example** : d0=784d_0 = 784d0​=784, d1=256d_1 = 256d1​=256, d2=128d_2 = 128d2​=128, d3=10d_3 = 10d3​=10 (a classifier for 28×2828 \times 2828×28 images into 10 classes):

params=(784×256+256)+(256×128+128)+(128×10+10)=235,146\text{params} = (784 \times 256 + 256) + (256 \times 128 + 128) + (128 \times 10 + 10) = 235{,}146params=(784×256+256)+(256×128+128)+(128×10+10)=235,146

**Dimension check** : always verify the matrix dimensions form a consistent chain:

z(l)⏟dl×1=W(l)⏟dl×dl−1⋅h(l−1)⏟dl−1×1+b(l)⏟dl×1\underbrace{\mathbf{z}^{(l)}}_{d_l \times 1} = \underbrace{\mathbf{W}^{(l)}}_{d_l \times d_{l-1}} \cdot \underbrace{\mathbf{h}^{(l-1)}}_{d_{l-1} \times 1} + \underbrace{\mathbf{b}^{(l)}}_{d_l \times 1}dl​×1z(l)​​=dl​×dl−1​W(l)​​⋅dl−1​×1h(l−1)​​+dl​×1b(l)​​
